# Events

## 1. Title
Zuva Life Events: Upcoming, Recurring, and Past

## 2. Category
Events

## 3. Detailed explanation
Zuva Life runs a rolling calendar of in-person and online experiences across its offering families (Spark Experiences, Zest Circle, Purpose Pathways). Events are the primary way people first encounter Zuva Life. Registrations are handled through the website event pages, Urbanaut, and (for the paid wealth workshop) Razorpay.

## 4. Upcoming events (as listed publicly)
- **Rethinking Wealth for Longer Lives** — One-day workshop, Friday 31 July, The Bangalore Room, Indiranagar. Rs. 9,500 excl. GST (early bird Rs. 8,000 excl. GST till 24 July). Register via Razorpay. See `workshops.md`.
- **Making Sense of Your Transitions** — 1.5-day guided residential retreat (Zest Circle). Register via the retreat page / Urbanaut. See `retreats.md`.
- **Build Your AI Inbox Assistant** — 2-hour online session. Register via Urbanaut. See `spark-experiences.md`.
- **Rethinking Work in Midlife** — Purpose Pathways session on fractional roles (using experience across organisations without being tied to one place). Register via Urbanaut.

> Dates and availability change. Confirm the current calendar on https://www.zuva.life/offerings and Zuva's Urbanaut profile.

## 5. Recurring events and formats
- Spark Experiences run on an ongoing basis (online and in person).
- Zest Circle themed experiences recur across the six themes.
- Community sessions and conversations via the WhatsApp community.
- **Webinars / online sessions:** Online Spark Experiences (e.g., AI-focused sessions) function as webinars. A dedicated recurring webinar series was **not explicitly found**; verify before claiming one.
