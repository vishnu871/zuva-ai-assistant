# Discovery Journey

## 1. Title
The Discovery Journey: A Structured 1:1 Process for Clarity

## 2. Category
Programs / One-on-One

## 3. Detailed explanation
Most people at this stage have more possibilities than they realise. The work is figuring out which ones are actually worth pursuing. The Discovery Journey is a structured one-on-one process that helps a person step back, take stock of their experience and strengths, and identify the possibilities most worth exploring further.

It is the recommended starting point for people who "don't arrive with a clear plan" but instead "arrive with a sense that something has shifted." The journey guides a self-reflection experience, exploring interests, values, and sense of purpose before moving into action. Together, Zuva Life and the participant look at experience, strengths, and current priorities to identify a few directions worth exploring further.

## 4. Key facts
- **Format:** One-on-one (1:1)
- **Length:** A 4 to 6 session journey
- **Purpose:** Clarify what matters and identify clear next steps into concrete pathways
- **Position:** The "Help Me Decide" entry point; feeds into Purpose Pathways
- **Page:** https://www.zuva.life/discovery

## 5. Services offered
Guided 1:1 reflection across interests, values, and purpose; stock-taking of experience and strengths; identification of a shortlist of directions and next steps.

## 6. Ideal participant / target audience
People in midlife who sense a shift but are not yet clear on direction, and who want a structured, personal way to think it through before acting.

## 7. Benefits
Clarity on what matters now, a manageable set of directions to explore, and confidence about next steps rather than an open-ended search.

## 8. Frequently asked questions
- **How many sessions is it?** A 4 to 6 session journey.
- **Is it group or individual?** Individual (1:1).
- **What if I already know my direction?** Consider Purpose Pathways instead.
- **What is the outcome?** A clearer sense of what matters and concrete next steps into pathways.
- **How do I begin?** Through the "Begin your Discovery Journey" / "Schedule a Discovery Journey" options on the site, or by contacting Zuva Life.

## 9. Important terminology
Self-reflection, values clarification, alignment, next steps, pathways.
