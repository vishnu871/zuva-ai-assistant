# Zuva Life Offerings Overview

## 1. Title
Offerings: Multiple Entry Points, One Connected Journey

## 2. Category
Programs / Services

## 3. Detailed explanation
People engage with Zuva Life in different ways, from short experiences and focused explorations to immersive cohort journeys. As priorities evolve, so do the possibilities. Someone may be considering a different way of working, exploring new avenues for contribution, or simply thinking more intentionally about the years ahead. Zuva Life offers several structured ways to explore these possibilities, all connected under the idea of designing the years ahead.

## 4. Key facts: the ways to engage
- **Spark Experiences** — Workshops, conversations, and small-group experiences that bring new ideas, perspectives, and possibilities into view. Often the entry point. See `spark-experiences.md`.
- **Purpose Pathways** — Focused direction tracks for those exploring advisory, entrepreneurial, creative, teaching, or portfolio roles, designed to help assess alignment and direction before a deeper commitment. See `purpose-pathways.md`.
- **Discovery Journey** — A structured 1:1 process (a 4 to 6 session journey) to help clarify what matters and identify clear next steps. The "Help Me Decide" starting point. See `discovery-journey.md`.
- **Zest Circle** — The signature cohort experience for people reflecting on what comes next in work, contribution, and life; combines reflective retreats and guided sessions. See `zest-circle.md`.
- **Community** — Ongoing connection, learning, and peer engagement (WhatsApp community and events). See `community.md`.
- **Partnering for Longer Lives (B2B)** — Zuva Life works with organisations rethinking how people live, work, and transition. See `events.md` and `contact.md` for engagement.

### Where do I begin?
- **Help Me Decide** — For those unsure what is next: start with the Discovery Journey, a self-reflection experience exploring interests, values, and sense of purpose before moving into action.
- **I Know What I Want** — For those already clear on a direction: explore Purpose Pathways and see how experience can be redeployed into meaningful next chapters.
- **Prefer to explore with others** — Join a Zest Circle cohort.

## 5. Services offered
Entry workshops, 1:1 clarity journeys, pathway exploration tracks, cohort programmes, retreats, community, and organisational partnerships.

## 6. Target audience
Midlife professionals at different stages of readiness, from "something has shifted" to "I know the direction I want to explore." See `audience.md`.

## 7. Benefits
A flexible on-ramp: people can start where they are and move between offerings as their thinking evolves.

## 8. Frequently asked questions
- **Where should I start if I have no clear plan?** Most people arrive with a sense that something has shifted rather than a plan. Start with the Discovery Journey.
- **Can I do just one workshop?** Yes. Spark Experiences are designed to stand on their own while opening up new perspectives.
- **Do the offerings connect?** Yes. They are multiple entry points into one connected journey.
- **Do you work with companies?** Yes, through the Partnering for Longer Lives track.
