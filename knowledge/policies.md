# Policies

## 1. Title
Zuva Life Policies (Privacy, Terms, Refund, Cancellation, Cookies)

## 2. Category
Policies / Legal

## 3. Detailed explanation
This file records the status of Zuva Life's public policies. As of capture, dedicated policy pages were not found on the website navigation. Where a policy is not publicly available, it is marked as such rather than invented. This is a gap worth closing for a production assistant, since users will ask about refunds and privacy.

## 4. Policy status
- **Privacy Policy:** Not publicly found on zuva.life at capture time. Note that the site is built on Wix and the newsletter/Substack processes data under Substack's own Terms, Privacy Policy, and Information Collection Notice. Payments via Razorpay and bookings via Urbanaut are governed by those providers' policies.
- **Terms of Service / Terms and Conditions:** Not publicly found on zuva.life. The website footer states "Copyright © 2026 Zuva Life - All Rights Reserved."
- **Refund Policy:** Not publicly found. Paid workshop pricing is shown excl. GST (e.g., Rethinking Wealth at Rs. 9,500 excl. GST, early bird Rs. 8,000 excl. GST till 24 July). Refund/transfer terms should be confirmed with Zuva Life directly.
- **Cancellation Policy:** Not publicly found. Confirm directly for each event.
- **Cookie Policy:** Not publicly found as a standalone page (Wix and third-party tools may set cookies).

## 5. What the assistant should do
When asked about refunds, cancellations, privacy, or cookies, the assistant should:
1. State that a formal published policy was not found on the site.
2. Direct the user to contact Zuva Life at info@zuvalife.com or +91 99454 10037 for the specific terms of their booking.
3. Note that payment and booking providers (Razorpay, Urbanaut) and the newsletter platform (Substack) apply their own policies.
Do not fabricate policy terms.

## 6. Frequently asked questions
- **Can I get a refund if I cancel?** A published refund policy was not found; please contact Zuva Life directly to confirm terms for your booking.
- **How is my data handled?** A published privacy policy was not found on the site; newsletter data is processed via Substack under its policies. Contact Zuva Life for specifics.
- **Do you charge GST?** Workshop prices are quoted exclusive of GST.
