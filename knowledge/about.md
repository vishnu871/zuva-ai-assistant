## 3. Detailed explanation
Zuva Life is a Bangalore-based venture creating structured pathways for people in midlife to stay relevant, engaged, and economically confident, now and in the years ahead. As people live and work longer, midlife is becoming an important turning point: careers extend, roles change, family responsibilities shift, and questions around relevance, financial continuity, contribution, and the years ahead take on greater importance.

Zuva Life works in the space of midlife and longevity, supporting people as they navigate transitions in work, identity, relationships, and life direction. It does this through workshops, cohort journeys, leadership conversations, retreats, and one-on-one journeys. The work focuses on longer careers, new forms of work and contribution, learning, financial continuity, and connection.

The tagline is "Designing the Years Ahead" and the site headline is "Your Best Years Are Still Ahead."

### The name
The name draws from "Zuv," a Kashmiri term of endearment that reflects care, connection, and the value of relationships. That spirit is central to how Zuva Life creates spaces for people to think, explore, and move forward.

### Vision
To help people remain engaged, relevant, and economically confident across longer lives, treating midlife as a powerful new chapter rather than a winding down. Zuva Life sees midlife as a period of reassessment and possibility.

### Mission
To create structured pathways, experiences, and communities that help people in midlife navigate what comes next in work, contribution, learning, and life, with clarity and intention.

### Story
For founder Vandana Srivastava, the question became personal. She watched her parents age in very different ways: one remained engaged, curious, and purposeful well into later life; the other, equally capable, gradually withdrew after stepping away from long-held roles and responsibilities. That contrast raised a question that stayed with her: what helps people remain engaged, connected, and able to contribute as life evolves? As she explored ageing and elder care, she came to realise that the quality of later life is often shaped much earlier than we think. That insight shifted the focus to midlife, the stage where many of the choices that influence the decades ahead are made. That question became the foundation for Zuva Life.

### Philosophy (summary)
The traditional model of education then career then retirement no longer reflects how most lives unfold. Longer lives create new possibilities for how people work, contribute, learn, and live. Zuva Life's founding insight is that people are not short of possibilities: they are short of pathways. See `our-approach.md` for the full philosophy.

### Values (as expressed in Zuva's public voice)
- Care and connection (rooted in the meaning of "Zuv")
- Agency and choice, not prescription
- Dignity of experience: people at this stage do not need motivation or reinvention, they need clarity, credible pathways, and room to think differently
- Reflection before action
- Building "a kinder world for everyone navigating midlife"
